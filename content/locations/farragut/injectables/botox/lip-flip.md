The Lip Flip is one of our most requested "add-on" treatments at Sarah Hitchcox
Aesthetics in Farragut. This quick procedure involves injecting a small amount
of [Botox](/farragut-botox) into the upper lip muscle, causing it to relax and
roll slightly outward. This creates the illusion of a fuller lip without adding
any actual volume, making it a perfect option for clients who want a subtle
enhancement or are hesitant about [lip filler](/farragut-filler/lip-filler).
Located on Parkside Drive, we help clients from Turkey Creek and beyond achieve
a cuter, poutier smile in just minutes.

While a Lip Flip is fantastic for a subtle change, many of our Farragut patients
choose to combine it with other treatments for a more dramatic result. If you
desire both shape and volume, we can perform a Lip Flip alongside
[lip filler](/farragut-filler/lip-filler). We can also address a
[gummy smile](/farragut-botox/gummy-smile) during the same session, ensuring
your smile is perfectly balanced.

This treatment is also excellent for smoothing vertical "smoker's lines" above
the lip. For deeper lines in this area, we might recommend pairing the Lip Flip
with [microneedling](/farragut-microneedling) or
[laser services](/farragut-laser-services) to resurface the skin. Whether you
are a student at UT or a professional in West Knox County, the Lip Flip is an
affordable, low-commitment way to enhance your natural beauty.
