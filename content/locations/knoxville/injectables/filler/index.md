Dermal fillers are a powerful tool for facial rejuvenation, capable of instantly
lifting, sculpting, and smoothing the face. At Sarah Hitchcox Aesthetics in
Knoxville, we specialize in using fillers to address the volume loss that
naturally occurs with age. Whether you are noticing hollows under your eyes,
flattening cheeks, or thinning lips, our skilled injectors on Kingston Pike can
craft a treatment plan to restore your youthful contours. As a key part of our
[Knoxville injectables](/knoxville-injectables) menu, fillers offer immediate
gratification with minimal downtime.

We offer a curated selection of premium hyaluronic acid fillers, including the
Juvederm and Restylane families. These versatile products allow us to treat a
variety of concerns with precision. While fillers restore volume, many patients
pair them with [Botox](/knoxville-botox) to smooth dynamic wrinkles for a
comprehensive anti-aging result. For those needing faster onset for event prep,
or perhaps looking for a slightly different neurotoxin profile, we also provide
[Dysport](/knoxville-dysport) and [Jeuveau](/knoxville-jeuveau) injections at
our West Knoxville clinic.

Our philosophy is "restoration, not alteration." We aim to enhance your natural
features, not change them. During your visit to our Bearden office, we might
also discuss [Kybella](/knoxville-kybella) if you have concerns about a double
chin, or [Skinvive](/knoxville-skinvive) if you want to improve overall skin
quality and hydration. By combining fillers with these other advanced
treatments, we can create a harmonious, balanced look that helps you feel
confident and revitalized. Trust your face to the experts at Sarah Hitchcox
Aesthetics.
