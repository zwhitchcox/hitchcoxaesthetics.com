## Brightening Under-Eyes with Filler in Knoxville

Dark circles and hollows under the eyes can make you look exhausted, regardless
of how much sleep you get. Under-eye filler, or tear trough filler, is a
specialized treatment we offer in Knoxville, TN to correct this volume deficit.
By placing a light, flexible filler in the hollows, we can smooth the transition
between the lower eyelid and the cheek, reflecting light better and instantly
brightening your appearance.

## A Rested, Refreshed Look

This is one of the most delicate areas to treat, requiring an expert hand. Sarah
Hitchcox uses advanced techniques to ensure safety and smooth results. For many
Knoxville patients, this single treatment provides the most dramatic "wowed"
reaction because it instantly wipes away signs of fatigue. It is a powerful tool
in our [dermal filler](/filler) toolkit.

## Blending with Cheek Volume

Tear trough hollows often coexist with volume loss in the mid-face. To achieve a
seamless result, we frequently pair under-eye treatment with
[cheek filler](/filler/cheek-filler). This supports the under-eye area and
creates a smooth, continuous contour from the lash line to the cheekbone. It is
the ultimate combination for refreshing the central face.
