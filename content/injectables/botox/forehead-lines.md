Horizontal forehead lines are among the most common reasons patients seek [Botox injections](/botox). These wrinkles develop gradually from years of raising the eyebrows, squinting, and making everyday expressions. Over time, what starts as dynamic movement lines can etch into permanent creases that make you look older or more stressed than you feel. Botox for forehead lines is the most effective non-surgical wrinkle treatment available to smooth forehead skin and restore a youthful, relaxed appearance.

## How Botox Smooths Horizontal Forehead Lines

Forehead wrinkles form when the frontalis muscle contracts repeatedly, folding the overlying skin into horizontal creases. [Botox](/botox) works by temporarily relaxing this muscle, preventing it from creating deep furrows while still allowing natural expression. Sarah places precise, measured injections across the forehead to soften lines without producing a frozen or expressionless look.

Most patients see visible smoothing within 3-7 days, with full results at the two-week mark. A forehead lines Botox session takes just 10-15 minutes, requires no downtime, and results last approximately 3-4 months.

## Why Forehead Botox Is an Essential Anti-Aging Treatment

Treating forehead wrinkles early is one of the smartest anti-aging strategies available. Regular Botox injections prevent dynamic lines from becoming deeply etched static wrinkles, meaning the skin stays smoother for longer over time. Patients who maintain consistent treatments often find they need fewer units as the frontalis muscle gradually weakens.

Forehead Botox pairs naturally with several other [cosmetic injections in the upper face](/injectables):

- [Crow's feet Botox](/botox/crows-feet) to reduce fine lines around the eyes
- A [non-surgical Botox brow lift](/botox/brow-lift) to open and elevate the eye area
