## Correcting Gummy Smiles in Knoxville

A "gummy smile" occurs when the upper lip elevates too high during smiling,
revealing excessive gum tissue. For patients who feel self-conscious about this,
surgery isn't the only option. At Sarah Hitchcox Aesthetics, we treat gummy
smiles in Knoxville, TN using precise Botox injections. By relaxing the muscles
that lift the upper lip, we can lower the lip line slightly, creating a more
balanced and confident smile.
